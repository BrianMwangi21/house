What are tags?
Key Value Pairs attached to AWS resources.

What is Metadata?
Data about data.

When are tags inherited? 
When tag resources create other resources.

What are examples where tags can be inherited? (3)
(1) AutoScaling; (2) CloudFormation; (3) EBS

What are resource groups?
Containers for tags. You can group resources that share one or more tags.

What information do Resource groups contain? (3) What does `contain` mean?
(1) Region (2) Name (3) Health Check; The data can be dumped to CSV via the console.

Can tags be nested?
Yes; For example `Develper = d1, Project = fiddle.sh`

Are tags applied to a resource group inherited by its resources?
No, the tags are not applied to group resources, but only the resource group itself.

What is the benefit of resource groups?
Its a great way of seeing who is consuming what.

What is tag editor used for?
It allows you to see all your resources and what they are tagged with. You can then add, update, or delete those tags.

What is the easiest way to find unused resources that you may need to delete? Why?
Tag Editor; Tag Editor goes across services. You do not need to access individual service dashboards to see resources.
